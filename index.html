Important TSA Rules

3-1-1 Liquids Rule

Carry-on liquids must be in containers of 3.4 oz (100ml) or less, all fitting in one quart-sized clear plastic bag.

Lithium Batteries

Spare lithium batteries and power banks must be in carry-on luggage only. Maximum 100Wh without approval.

Sharp Objects

Scissors under 4 inches allowed in carry-on. All knives must be checked. Tools under 7 inches generally allowed.
